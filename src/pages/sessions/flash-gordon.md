eight 300m efforts, then one 800m effort to finish.

* **800m effort** (two laps of the track)
* 400m jog recovery (one lap of the track)

* **300m effort** (three-quarters of a lap)
* 100m jog recovery (one-quarter of a lap)
* **300m effort** (three-quarters of a lap)
* 100m jog recovery (one-quarter of a lap)
