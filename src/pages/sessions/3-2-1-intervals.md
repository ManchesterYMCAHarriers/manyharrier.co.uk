This is a timed session, with intervals marked by a whistle. We do 3 sets of the following pattern, so the session 
lasts for 30 minutes in total.

- **3 minutes effort**
- 1 minute walk/jog recovery
- **2 minutes effort**\
- 1 minute walk/jog recovery
- **1 minute effort**
- 2 minutes walk/jog recovery
